Camelmark by mutewinter

A boomarklet to look up items from Amazon.com and Newegg.com on camelcamelcamel and camelegg.

Install It

Desktop

Drag Camelmark into your bookmarks toolbar. You're done.

Mobile

Make a new bookmark for this page. Copy this into the URL field.

javascript:%20((function%20()%20{var%20s%20=%20document.createElement(%27s%27+%27cr%27+%27ipt%27);s.setAttribute(%27type%27,%20%27text/javascript%27);s.setAttribute(%27charset%27,%20%27UTF-8%27);s.setAttribute(%27src%27,%20%27https://rawgit.com/mutewinter/Camelmark/master/camelmark.js%27);document.documentElement.appendChild(s);})());

Usage

  1. Go to a product on Amazon.com or Newegg.com
  2. Click Camelmark
  3. Profit
